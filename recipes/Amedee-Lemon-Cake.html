Amedee Lemon (and lime) Cake

My sister's lemon cake at the deli <3

Lemon cake may sound like a fairly easy and simple recipe but in facts it is extremly hard. Harder than any dark soukls game...

(btw the flapjack are indeed $285 lol)

Ingredients

  • 175g Butter
  • 175g Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 60g Ground Almonds
  • 120g Flour
  • 1tsp Baking powder
  • 1 Lemon and 1 Lime zest
  • 5tbsp Milk
  • 45g Sugar
  • 1 Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Mix sugar + zest and the eggs
  2. Add rest off the ingredients and bake for 45/50 min
  3. Boil the sugar and juice and pour on the baked cake
