Amedee Lemon (and lime) Cake

My sister's lemon cake at the deli <3

Lemon cake may sound like a fairly easy and simple recipe but my sister carefully tested this recipe many times to find the prefect way of making it, and it never fails !

(btw the flapjack are indeed $285 lol)

Ingredients

  • 175g Butter
  • 175g Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 60g Ground Almonds
  • 120g Flour
  • 1tsp Baking powder
  • 1 Lemon and 1 Lime zest
  • 5tbsp Milk
  • 45g Sugar
  • 1 Lemon juice
