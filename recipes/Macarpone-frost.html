Not my sister's mascarpone frosting on a cupycake <3

Everyone knows how to make a frosting, but does everyone knows how to make a banana frosting out of Mascarpone ? Absolutly not, and Haruki Murakami is one of the proof.

Ingredients

  • 600ml Heavy cream, I think
  • 140g Mascarpone
  • 2tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Large eggs
  • Sugar, as much as you want

Steps

  1. Mix sugar + zest and the eggs
  2. Add rest off the ingredients and bake for 45/50 min
  3. Boil the sugar and juice and pour on the baked cake

Crazy right when you know how to make a lemon cake, you can make anything !
